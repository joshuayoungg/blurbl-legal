We're glad you're reading with us.

Need help?

The fastest way to reach us is right inside the app:

Open Settings → Feedback to request a feature, report a problem, or ask a question. We read every message.

Email

Prefer email? Write to us at support@myorbis.me and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

  • How do I read a book? Tap any book in your library and tap to move through the conversation, bubble by bubble.
  • How do I create or remix a story? Choose a book and pick Remix, or create an original interactive story and chat with the characters.
  • How do I delete my account? Open Settings → Delete account. This permanently removes your data.
  • How do I report content? In any interactive story, tap the flag icon in the top bar to report it.
